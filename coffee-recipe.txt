Ingredients:-

1 tbs Nescafe Coffee
2 tbs sugar
1 cup milk
1/4 cup water



Directions:-

1. First pour the water in a heating vessel and heat for half a minute.
2. Add milk to it.
3. Add coffee and sugar.
4. Stir properly and mix all the ingredients.
5. Heat the mixture at the medium level.
6. After about 2 minutes, switch off the gas and carefully pour the steaming
   coffee in a glass.
7. Enjoy the refreshing drink with a plate of biscuits and other snacks.
